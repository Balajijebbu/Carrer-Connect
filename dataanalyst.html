Data Analyst Job

Join our team as a Data Analyst and make an impact!




Job Overview

We are looking for a skilled Data Analyst to analyze large datasets and provide actionable insights. You will work closely with our team to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for improvement.

Company: TCS

Average Salary:15 LPA

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and analyze data from various sources.
  • Generate reports and visualizations to communicate findings.
  • Identify data trends and provide recommendations for decision-making.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive data-driven decisions.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., Computer Science, Statistics).
  • Proven experience in data analysis and visualization.
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools (e.g., Python, SQL, Excel).
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
